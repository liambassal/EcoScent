The EcoPod System

A beautifully designed frosted glass bottle that lasts a lifetime. Just drop in a compostable refill pod, add water, and you have premium concentrated liquid detergent. Zero plastic waste, 100% clean clothes.

Your Signature Scent

Why smell like a generic supermarket aisle? Choose from our curated library of fine fragrances, or send us a sample of your favorite perfume and we will replicate it perfectly for your laundry.

Pure Comfort

Plant-based enzyme technology lifts the toughest stains while remaining gentle on your skin. Experience the joy of burying your face in freshly washed linens that smell exactly how you want them to.

Delivered to Your Door

Never run out of detergent again. Our subscription box delivers your personalized scent pods straight to your mailbox right when you need them. Housed in 100% recycled, compostable packaging.
